Undo the Flu

You can make a difference. With a simple test kit at home, you can help us gain valuable knowledge about the virus, how it spreads, and how to protect people in the future.


Why This Matters

How to participate

Have flu-like symptoms? Visit your General Practitioner (GP)

If eligible, your GP will give you a test kit to complete at home

Download the flu@home app. It will lead you through the test steps

The app will guide you through reading your flu test results

Why This Matters

We know it’s lousy to feel sick – but with this test you’re helping to track the flu and learn whether people (like you) can test themselves at home. We’re on to something great and need your help. Join the University of Adelaide, University of Washington Medicine, and Audere as we undo the flu.
